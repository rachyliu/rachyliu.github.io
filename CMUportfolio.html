Who am I?

Hello, my name is Ruiqing (Rachel) Liu. I am the Lead User Interface Developer in Truelearn, implementing stylized ASP.NET web application with HTML front end. I am working with JavaScript, CSS, html and libraries like jQuery, angularJS, KendoUI.

I was a graduate student in the Master of Entertainment Technology program from the Entertainment Technology Center(ETC) in the Carnegie Mellon University(CMU).I learned art and able to work with Photoshop, Illustrator and Maya from the program. I can make 3D models, rigging and especially 3D animation.

I got Information System Bachelor's Degree from Arizona State University, which allow me to work with a good understanding of programming logic. I had experience using C#, Python and SQL.

Hungry Donkey Game Project

(Entertainment Technology Center in Carnegie Mellon University)

*Cross-platform multiplayer racing game
*BVW Round 5 Project: Designed and developed by six CMU ETC students in 3 weeks. Racing to win the farm! Rock the donkey to run. Audiences can support by joining teams and throwing carrots to the other rider's donkey to slow them down. Cover your donkeys eyes to keep it running when carrots appear in your way, otherwise the donkeys will stop and begin to eat.
